Places to Visit

Yanwath Hall

A quarter of a mile from the house - one of the best preserved peel towers in England.


Mayburgh Henge

1 mile from the house, in the direction of Penrith. Owned by Englis Heritage.


Brougham Castle

2 miles from the house. Mostly ruined castle owned by English Heritage.


Rheged

Indoor centre with shopping and activities.


Dalemain House and Gardens

Historic mansion and gardens - venue of the famous Marmalade Festival.


Lowther Castle

Gardens and adventure playground.


Ullswater Steamers

Cruise Ullswater from Pooley Bridge, with the option of getting off in various places around the lake.
