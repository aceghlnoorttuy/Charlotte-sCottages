Ryebank Cottage

Document

Ryebank Cottage is a cosy two-bedroom, traditional stone house. It has a beautiful, grassy garden with seating area. The bathroom has a bath and shower. The kitchen is well equipped and there's a dining area in there. The lounge is double-height, with a multi-fuel stove for those chilly days. There's a shop and pub within 300m. Up to date availability can be found on the calendar on the Airbnb website, linked to below. I can offer you a better price if you then contact me directly to book.
