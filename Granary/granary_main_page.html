The Granary

Document

The Granary is a cosy one-bedroom, upper-floor apartment. It has a garden with seating area, including a shed which is ideal for storing outdoor equipment. The bathroom has a bath and shower. The kitchen is well equipped and there's a dining and lounge area, with a window seat which offers a lovely view of the hills. There's a village shop and pub within 300 metres.
